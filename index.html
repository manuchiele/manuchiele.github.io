My Website for Web Design Class

When I was younger, about 3-4 years old, I would stay with a babysitter while my parents worked. My mom would pick me up from the babysitter at the end of her workday. At the time, she did not own a car, so she would bike to work and to pick me up in her bike. She would put me in my little seat, which was right in front of her, with basket right in front of me. She says that on the way home, we would bike past trees and I would make her stop at each tree so I could get a leaf from it and place it in the basket. I do not remember this at all, but I remember being 10-11 years old and walking home from school and doing the same thing without even knowing it was something that I did when I was just a baby.
